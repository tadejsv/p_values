About

This webapp enables the calculation of p-rationality values based on a paper by Fabrizio Germano and Peio Zuazo-Garin1 titled Bounded rationality and correlated equilibria. The uses the linprog module of the scipy package to check for the existence of a solution for a given p value, and uses simple bisection to approximate the upper bound.

  1. Germano, F. & Zuazo-Garin, P. Int J Game Theory (2017) 46: 595. [Link] ↩︎
